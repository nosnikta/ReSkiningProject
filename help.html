General Information

You should possess or have frequent access to prescribed texts as you need to study these in detail. Recommended texts are books which may be useful with your studies.

In some cases textbook information has yet to be available. The Subject Outline will contain full details of requirements; alternaively you may contact the University Co-Operative Bookshop at Bathurst. Student Central can also provide further information.

Purchasing Information

Textbooks may be purchased from any source available to you, however all Distance Education textbooks are normally available by web order from the University Co-Operative Bookshop and supplied by the Co-Operative Bookshop at Bathurst, or by phone on (02) 6332 3722. For all internal students you can find your text requirements using the University Co-Operative Bookshop site and then follow the textbook search instructions.
